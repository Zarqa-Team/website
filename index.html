Introduction

Zarqa Team is an ambitious project launched by the InoNest community at Yildiz Technical University (YTU), an international technology-focused group that emphasizes practical education and innovative projects. InoNest empowers international students through hands-on learning experiences, including engineering workshops and participation in prestigious competitions like Teknofest. It fosters a multicultural environment that brings students together from diverse backgrounds to achieve academic excellence and drive innovation.

Zarqa Team is preparing to participate in the 2025 Teknofest Combat UAV Competition, where teams develop advanced unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) designed for combat simulation scenarios. We invite your esteemed organization to join us as a sponsor to support this remarkable journey.

About the Team

As international students, our team members have encountered challenges in adapting to the Turkish academic and professional environment. Zarqa Team was founded to bridge the communication gap between Turkish and English speakers, fostering collaboration and inclusion. By primarily conducting research and communication in English, we get the advantage of recruiting members from all nationalities and having wider access to information. We aim to challenge our engineering abilities and academic knowledge.

Our team's name and logo come from the Arabian legend of Zarqaa' Al Yamama, a character who is said to have blue eyes and a gaze that can see the distance of one week, making her a pillar of defense in her city.

About the Project

Teknofest is an international technology competition that has been organized since 2018 in collaboration with the T3 Foundation and the Turkish Aerospace Industries (TAI). The Combat UAV Competition challenges teams to develop and test innovative UAVs in simulated combat scenarios, enhancing youth interest in technology and advancing their skills for future achievements.

As part of the InoNest community, our team consists of skilled specialists in programming, mechanics, electronics, control systems, and UAV development, ensuring a well-rounded approach to innovation. Your support will enable the Zarqa Team to bridge the gap between academia and industry, fostering innovation and inspiring the next generation of engineers.

Team Objectives & Expected Outcomes

  • Develop an innovative combat UAV capable of competing in the Teknofest competition.
  • Compete at the highest level to establish our presence and recognition in the UAV technology sector.
  • Enhance the technical and engineering skills of participating students, preparing them for careers in leading technology companies.
  • Contribute to the advancement of UAV technology, fostering a stronger industry-academia connection.

Project Impact on Students & Industry

More than just a technical challenge, our project harnesses cutting-edge technology and expertise to make a lasting impact on both academia and industry

  • Promoting Innovation & Scientific Research: Turkey's thriving technological ecosystem provides the perfect environment for pioneering research and development efforts.
  • Skill Development & Capacity Building: Students will gain technical and engineering expertise, equipping them for professional roles in Turkey's technology sector.
  • Educational & Social Impact: Raising awareness of the importance of science and technology within the community and encouraging more young people to pursue careers in these fields.
  • Fostering Teamwork & Collaboration: The project brings together students from different disciplines, reinforcing a collaborative and innovative mindset.

Our ultimate goal is to establish Zarqa' Team as a leader in UAV technology in Turkey, effectively utilizing available resources and knowledge to contribute to scientific advancement and societal development.

Team Members & Achievements
